10. Repo Hygiene Rules
Every serious change gets a branch.
A PR must have: single purpose, small scope, tests, no generated bytecode, no data files, no unrelated drift.
Merge only when: pytest passes, changed files are expected, no production write risk, no lookahead risk, no duplicated strategy truth without comment.

11. Indicator Research Appendix
Claude is right to warn against adding indicator combos before reproduction. Indicator research is allowed only after Gate 2, and ideally after Gate 3. The goal is not to bolt on RSI/MACD until winrate rises. The goal is to test whether a small number of conceptually aligned confirmations improve robustness without overfit.

Allowed later research families:
A. Exhaustion confirmation:
- RSI divergence near rolling low
- ATR/ADR stretch normalization
- declining volume after extreme push
- candle rejection / close-quality confirmation

B. Order-flow confirmation:
- CVD divergence
- delta exhaustion
- negative delta into lower low with failed continuation
- OFI only when real coverage exists

C. Exit refinement:
- volatility-scaled time exit
- ATR emergency stop
- MFE/MAE-informed protective stop
- no TP/SL curve fitting; Phase 1H already showed TP/SL can clip edge

D. Regime boundary improvement:
- improve EXHAUSTED label, not random confluence stacking
- compare central regime classifier against replay fallback parity

Anti-overfit rules:
- no huge combinatorial sweeps
- no optimizing thresholds before reproduction
- require walk-forward, de-overlap, yearly split, and cost stress
- improvement must hold at cost12 and preferably cost20
- 2025 cannot be hidden by an overfit no-trade filter

Do not build the whole trading firm at once. Confirm one edge. Reproduce it. Stress it. Paper trade it. Then add the next layer.
